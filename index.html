What is Morshian About?

Moshcoin

We started the cryptocurrency Moshcoin. We believe in business without borders, trade throughout the galaxy. You don’t have to worry about slow transactions while governments convert their currency because Moshcoin is accepted everywhere. Moshcoin means direct payment between buyer and seller, so you don't pay taxes nor leave records.

Minerals

Our autonomous machines can refine minerals like diamonds, gold, nickel, and copper to full 100% concentration in minutes.

Biodiversity

We have the greatest respect for all life. We deliver 500 million cubic kilometers of water to life centers annually. We are promoting healthy planetary climates and reversing the endangerment of species. We fuel agriculture so all creatures have a chance to live. We do not discriminate on intelligence, galactic origin, nor dysanthropomorphity.

Time

Morshian is also part of the XFR, the galactic organization which standardizes galactic time. We supply our computing power to ensure that everyone has free access to reliable time no matter where they are in the galaxy.
